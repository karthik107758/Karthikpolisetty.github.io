## üìä Business Solutions & Key Dashboards
The core focus was turning complex field sales data into actionable KPIs for management and sales directors.
### "Sell in Sell Out Dashboard"
* Purpose: Provided visibility into the gap between product sold to distributors (Sell-In) and product sold to end customers (Sell-Out).
* Impact: Enabled the business to investigate the gaps between primary vs secondary sales, leading to better inventory management and campaign alignment.
### "Sales Force Effectiveness Dashboard"
* Purpose: Measured the individual performance of field sales agents (e.g., visits, sales targets, route efficiency).
* Impact: The insights helped management plan proper routing to improve sales and provided clear metrics to reward high-performing individuals with incentives.
